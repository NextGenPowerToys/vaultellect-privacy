How to play

Each vault shows four emoji picture-magnets. Read them, deduce the year in history they point to (BC or AD), and dial the code. Solving earns points; wrong tries and skips cost points. Reveal a clue with points or by watching an ad.

Frequently asked questions

My progress disappeared / I got a new phone.

Progress is saved locally on your device and is not tied to an account, so it does not transfer between devices or survive an uninstall. Reinstalling on the same device keeps existing progress.

How do I change the language?

Tap the globe icon in the top-left corner at any time. Vaultellect supports English, Russian, Hebrew, Italian, and French.

Why am I seeing ads, and can I control them?

Ads (via Google AdMob) keep the game free. On iOS you are asked for tracking permission the first time; in some regions a consent form lets you control personalized ads. You can change these choices in your device settings. See our Privacy Policy.

The vault locked down / I ran out of tries.

After several wrong entries the dial pauses briefly. Wait out the short timer for free, or watch a short ad to unlock immediately and reveal a clue — it is never a dead end.

I found a wrong answer or a bug.

Please email us the topic and what you saw at nextgenpowertoys@gmail.com and we will fix it.
