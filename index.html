Privacy Policy — Vaultellect

Last updated: 5 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Vaultellect ("the App", "we", "us"), published by NextGenPowerToys, handles information. By using the App you agree to this policy.

Information the App collects directly

The App does not require an account and does not ask you for personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. Your game progress (score, solved puzzles, current puzzle, language choice, sound setting) is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Advertising and third-party data collection

The App displays non-personalized ads through Google AdMob. The App does not track you across other apps or websites and does not use your iOS advertising identifier (IDFA) for tracking. To serve and measure these ads, Google and its partners may still process limited information such as coarse location derived from your IP address, device type and settings, and ad-interaction data — used for showing ads, frequency capping, and fraud prevention rather than behavioural targeting.

This processing is carried out by Google, not by us. For details on how Google uses this data, see Google's Advertising policy and Google's Privacy Policy.

Your consent and choices

  • Consent for ads (UMP): where required (e.g. in the EEA/UK), the App shows a Google-provided consent form so you can manage your advertising choices.
  • You can reset or limit your device's advertising identifier at any time in your device settings.

Children's privacy

The App is a general-audience game and is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will address it.

Data retention

Because game data is stored locally on your device, you can remove it at any time by deleting the App. Data collected by Google for advertising is retained according to Google's own policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
